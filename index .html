Instructions for Catastrophic Spaces

Instructions

Roll the cube on the board and choose the icon indicated from the website.

Each side of the cube has a number and an arrow.

The arrow indicates direction, the number is number of spaces away. Continue until you have entered all the spaces.

Catastrophic Spaces by Chris Godber and Becca Smith 2017
